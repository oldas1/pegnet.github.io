Why PegNet?

Frictionless trading platform

Borderless. Self-issue tokens. Without need for counterparty, collateral, or smart contracts. Accessible.

The most efficient exchange of assets

Straight, honest exchange rates. Unlimited without broker fees, no slippage. No market makers. No middle man. $5 trillion gets traded even day across currency, stocks and funds. How much gets lost in conversion to a middle man?

Pay without the cost

Pay merchants in pegged local currency 1:1 ratio. Frictionless payment rail. Suitable for micropayments.

Save money and trees

90% less energy than Bitcoin and Ethereum with none of the limitations. A better Store of Value. Unlimited data recorded at high TPS, with ﬁxed low cost transactions. 1000 tx for $1 - ideal for micropayments.
